Why We Serve

Hearthan exists to bring warmth, nourishment, and heartfelt support to elders and families in need. We believe no one should feel forgotten, especially in the second half of the month when assistance tends to fade. Our mission is to serve our community with kindness by providing meals, supplies, and a sense of home to those who need it most.
